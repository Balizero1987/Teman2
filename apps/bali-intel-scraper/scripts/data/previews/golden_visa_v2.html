Executive Summary

Indonesia has officially launched its highly anticipated Golden Visa program, offering long-term residency of up to 10 years for foreign investors who meet specific investment thresholds. This landmark immigration policy represents a major shift in Indonesia's approach to attracting high-net-worth individuals.

Investment Tiers

  • Tier 1 (5-Year Visa): USD 350,000 minimum investment in government bonds or approved sectors
  • Tier 2 (10-Year Visa): USD 700,000 minimum investment with additional business establishment
  • Corporate Track: Companies investing USD 2.5M+ can sponsor up to 10 employees

Key Benefits

  • Multiple Entry: Unlimited entries during visa validity
  • Work Authorization: Full work rights included (no separate KITAS needed)
  • Family Inclusion: Spouse and children under 18 included
  • Path to PR: Eligible for KITAP (permanent residency) after 5 years
  • No Minimum Stay: Unlike retirement visa, no 6-month residency requirement

Eligible Investment Sectors

The Ministry of Investment (BKPM) has approved the following sectors:

  • Technology and digital startups
  • Tourism infrastructure (hotels, resorts)
  • Renewable energy projects
  • Healthcare and pharmaceuticals
  • Education facilities
  • Manufacturing and export industries

Application Process

Applications can be submitted through:

  1. Directorate General of Immigration (online portal)
  2. Indonesian embassies/consulates abroad
  3. Authorized immigration agents

Processing time: 30-45 business days

What is the minimum investment for Indonesia's Golden Visa?

The minimum investment is USD 350,000 for a 5-year visa, or USD 700,000 for a 10-year visa.

Can my family be included in the Golden Visa?

Yes, your spouse and children under 18 are included at no additional investment requirement.

Do I need a separate work permit with the Golden Visa?

No, the Golden Visa includes full work authorization. You don't need a separate KITAS.
