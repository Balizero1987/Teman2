Indonesia Launches New Golden Visa Program for High-...

January 04, 2026 1 min read Unknown

Executive Summary

Indonesia has officially launched its highly anticipated Golden Visa program, offering long-term residency to foreign investors who meet specific investment thresholds. This landmark immigration policy aims to attract wealthy individuals and boost foreign direct investment.

Key Requirements

  • Minimum Investment: USD 350,000 in Indonesian government bonds or approved business sectors
  • Validity: 5-10 years depending on investment level
  • Benefits: Multiple entry, work authorization, path to permanent residency
  • Processing Time: 30-45 business days

Who Qualifies?

The program targets:

  • High-net-worth individuals with proven investment capacity
  • Business owners looking to establish presence in Southeast Asia
  • Retirees seeking long-term residency in Bali

Impact for Expats

This visa represents a significant shift in Indonesia's immigration policy, previously known for complex and restrictive visa requirements. The Golden Visa offers a streamlined path for those willing to make substantial investments.

How to Apply

Applications can be submitted through the Directorate General of Immigration or authorized visa agents. Required documents include proof of funds, clean criminal record, and valid passport.

--- Source: Ministry of Law and Human Rights Press Release
