## Limitations & Ethics

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Important Considerations">

**Current Limitations:**
- Specific real people (needs training)
- Exact brand logos (legal issues)
- Complex scenes with many subjects
- Motion/action sequences

**Ethical Concerns:**
- Deepfake potential
- Artist displacement
- Copyright ambiguity
- Misinformation risk

**Best Practice:**
Always disclose AI-generated imagery in professional contexts.

</InfoCard>
