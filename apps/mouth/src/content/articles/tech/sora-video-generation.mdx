# Sora: Text to Video at Scale

After a year of previews and limited access, **OpenAI's Sora** is now publicly available. The implications for content creation, marketing, and storytelling are profound.

---

## What Sora Can Do

<InfoCard variant="highlight" title="Current Capabilities">

- **Duration**: Up to 60 seconds per clip
- **Resolution**: Up to 1080p
- **Physics**: Realistic motion and gravity
- **Characters**: Consistent people across scenes
- **Styles**: Photorealistic to animated
- **Camera**: Complex movements (tracking, drone, crane)

</InfoCard>

---

## Practical Applications

### Marketing Videos
Generate promotional content for your business without production crews. Hotel walkthrough? Product demo? Customer testimonial style? All possible with text prompts.

### Training & Onboarding
Create professional training videos at a fraction of traditional cost. Visualize complex processes, demonstrate procedures, show best practices.

### Social Media Content
Produce platform-optimized content daily. Vertical for TikTok/Reels, square for Instagram, widescreen for YouTube — all from the same prompt.

### Real Estate Tours
Generate virtual property tours for listings. Show potential at golden hour, rainy season, fully furnished — before the property exists.

## The Indonesia Angle

Sora excels at generating:

- **Tropical Landscapes** — Rice terraces, beaches, jungles
- **Cultural Scenes** — Ceremonies, traditional dance
- **Urban Life** — Jakarta skyline, Bali streets
- **Architecture** — Modern villas, traditional compounds

The model has been trained extensively on Indonesian visual content, resulting in authentic representations.

---

## Limitations to Know

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Current Constraints">

**Technical:**
- Complex character interactions can glitch
- Text in videos remains unreliable
- Hands still occasionally problematic
- Processing time: 5-15 minutes per clip

**Practical:**
- Cannot use real people's faces
- Brand logos need careful handling
- Music must be added separately
- No audio generation (yet)

</InfoCard>

## Getting Started

For businesses new to AI video:

1. **Start with ChatGPT Plus** — Low barrier entry
2. **Master Prompting** — Describe shots cinematically
3. **Build a Library** — Create reusable clips
4. **Combine with Traditional** — Hybrid workflows
5. **Iterate Rapidly** — Generate many, select best
