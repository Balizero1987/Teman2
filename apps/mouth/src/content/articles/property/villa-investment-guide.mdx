## Rental Licensing

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Rental License Required">
Operating a rental villa requires:
- **Pondok Wisata** license for small accommodations
- **TDUP** for larger operations
- Tourism registration
- Various local permits

Operating without licenses risks fines, closure, and visa problems.
</InfoCard>

### License Requirements

| License | For | Issued By |
|---------|-----|-----------|
| Pondok Wisata | Small guesthouses | Local government |
| TDUP | Tourism accommodation | Tourism dept |
| NIB | Business registration | OSS |
| Environmental | Operations impact | Environment dept |
