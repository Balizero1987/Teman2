## Visa Options

### Best Visas for Nomads

| Visa | Duration | Best For |
|------|----------|----------|
| VOA | 30+30 days | Short stays |
| E33G | Up to 1 year | Digital Nomad Visa |
| Second Home | 5 years | Long-term, wealthy |
| Remote Worker | Special category | Working remotely |

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="Visa Strategy">
**Common nomad approach:**

1. Enter on VOA (30 days)
2. Extend once (+30 days)
3. If staying longer, exit to Singapore
4. Return with new VOA or apply for E33G Digital Nomad Visa

Many nomads do "visa runs" every 60 days or get the E33G Digital Nomad Visa for long-term stays.
</InfoCard>

## Internet Situation

### Where You'll Get Good Internet

| Location | Speed | Reliability |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| Coworking spaces | 50-200 Mbps | Excellent |
| Modern villas | 20-100 Mbps | Good |
| Cafes (tourist areas) | 10-50 Mbps | Variable |
| Rural areas | Variable | Less reliable |

### Tips for Reliable Work

| Tip | Why |
|-----|-----|
| Coworking for calls | Best reliability |
| Backup mobile data | Failover option |
| Test before renting | Verify speed |
| Avoid peak hours | For video calls |
| Choose fiber if possible | More stable |

## Scooter Essentials

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Scooter Safety">
**Important:**

- International Driving Permit needed (or Indo license)
- Helmet always (it's the law)
- Insurance recommended
- Avoid riding at night drunk (common accidents)
- Learn local traffic patterns
- Rent or buy long-term

Scooter accidents are the #1 risk for nomads in Bali.
</InfoCard>

### Scooter Costs

| Option | Cost |
|--------|------|
| Daily rental | IDR 70-100K |
| Monthly rental | IDR 800K-1.5M |
| Annual rental | IDR 8-15M |
| Buy used | IDR 10-20M |

## Common Challenges

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Be Prepared For">
**Reality check:**

- **Internet outages** - Backup plan needed
- **Scooter accidents** - Be careful
