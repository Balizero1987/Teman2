## Arbitration

### Why Arbitration?

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="Benefits of Arbitration">
**For commercial disputes:**

- **Expert arbitrators** - Industry knowledge
- **Confidential** - No public record
- **Faster** - Than courts usually
- **Enforceable** - International treaties
- **Flexible** - Parties choose process
- **Final** - Limited appeal grounds

Most significant commercial contracts include arbitration clauses.
</InfoCard>

### BANI (Indonesian Arbitration)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Full name | Badan Arbitrase Nasional Indonesia |
| Location | Jakarta, Surabaya |
| Rules | BANI Rules |
| Language | Indonesian or English |
| Costs | Filing fee + arbitrator fees |
| Timeline | 6-12 months typical |

### International Options

| Institution | When Used |
|-------------|-----------|
| SIAC (Singapore) | International contracts |
| ICC | Large international deals |
| HKIAC | Asia-Pacific focus |
| LCIA | UK-related matters |

<InfoCard variant="note" title="Arbitration Clause">
**Standard arbitration clause:**

"Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this agreement shall be finally resolved by arbitration under the BANI Rules. The seat of arbitration shall be Jakarta. The language of arbitration shall be English."

Include this in your contracts!
</InfoCard>

## Enforcement

### Enforcing Arbitration Awards

<InfoCard variant="note" title="International Enforcement">
**Indonesia is party to:**
- New York Convention (1958)
- ICSID Convention

Foreign arbitration awards are enforceable in Indonesia through court registration process (typically 2-4 months).

Indonesian BANI awards are directly enforceable.
</InfoCard>

### Enforcing Court Judgments

| Situation | Process |
|-----------|---------|
| Indonesian judgment | Direct execution |
| Foreign judgment | Must re-litigate in Indonesia |

Foreign court judgments are **not directly enforceable** - must sue again in Indonesian courts.
