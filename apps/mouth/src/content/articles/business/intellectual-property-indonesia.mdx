## Trademark Registration

### Why Register in Indonesia?

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="First-to-File System">
Indonesia uses **first-to-file** system:
- First to register owns the trademark
- Your trademark elsewhere doesn't protect you here
- "Squatters" may register your brand first
- Without registration, limited enforcement

**Register your trademark before entering Indonesia market!**
</InfoCard>

## Copyright Protection

### Automatic Protection

Copyright protection is **automatic** in Indonesia for:
- Literary works
- Music and songs
- Art and photographs
- Films and videos
- Software and databases
- Architecture

### Why Register Anyway?

| Benefit | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|
| Evidence | Proves ownership date |
| Enforcement | Easier legal action |
| Licensing | Clearer ownership |
| Asset value | For business valuation |

### Registration Process

| Step | Duration |
|------|----------|
| Prepare documents | 1 week |
| Online application | 1 day |
| DGIP review | 1-3 months |
| Certificate issued | 1-2 weeks |

## Trade Secrets

### Protection Requirements

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="Protect Your Secrets">
To maintain trade secret protection:

1. **Identify** what's confidential
2. **Mark** documents as confidential
3. **Limit** access to need-to-know
4. **NDAs** with employees and partners
5. **Security** measures (physical and digital)
6. **Training** staff on confidentiality

Once public, protection is lost forever.
</InfoCard>

### NDA Essentials

| Element | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Definition | What's confidential |
| Obligations | How to protect |
| Duration | How long binding |
| Exceptions | Permitted disclosures |
| Remedies | Breach consequences |
| Jurisdiction | Indonesian law typically |

## International Protection

### Treaties Indonesia Follows

| Treaty | Benefit |
|--------|---------|
| Paris Convention | Priority claims (12 months) |
| TRIPS | Minimum standards |
| Madrid Protocol | International TM system |
| PCT | International patent filing |
| Berne Convention | Copyright recognition |

<InfoCard variant="note" title="Priority Claims">
If you have existing IP registration abroad, you can claim **priority** in Indonesia:
- Trademarks: 6 months from first filing
- Patents: 12 months from first filing

This backdates your Indonesian filing.
</InfoCard>
