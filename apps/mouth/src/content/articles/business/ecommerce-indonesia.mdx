## Legal Requirements

### For Foreign Investors

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="PT PMA Required">
**Foreign e-commerce investment:**

- PT PMA needed for significant operations
- Minimum investment requirements
- KBLI codes for e-commerce activities
- NIB through OSS
- May need PPMSE registration

Cannot just operate informally as foreigner.
</InfoCard>

## Tax Obligations

### E-Commerce Tax

| Tax | Rate | When |
|-----|------|------|
| Corporate income | 22% | Annual profit |
| VAT (PPN) | 11% | If PKP, on sales |
| Withholding | Various | On payments made |
| Platform fees | No VAT charged | Commission to platform |

<InfoCard variant="note" title="VAT on E-Commerce">
**VAT considerations:**

- Threshold: IDR 4.8B revenue
- Must register as PKP
- Issue e-Faktur
- Platform may handle for marketplace sales
- Cross-border has different rules
</InfoCard>
