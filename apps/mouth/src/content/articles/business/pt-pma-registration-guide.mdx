# How to Register a PT PMA in Indonesia: Step by Step 2025

Registering a PT PMA (Perseroan Terbatas Penanaman Modal Asing) is the standard way for foreigners to establish a legal business entity in Indonesia. Here's exactly how to do it.

## Quick Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Timeline** | 4-8 weeks |
| **Min Investment** | IDR 10 billion (~$625,000) |
| **Min Shareholders** | 2 (can be foreign) |
| **Min Directors** | 1 (can be foreign) |
| **Min Commissioners** | 1 (can be foreign) |

## Tips for Success

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="Registration Tips">

2. **Plan capital**: Know how you'll deploy IDR 10B
3. **Use professionals**: Corporate secretary saves headaches
4. **Timeline**: Allow 6-8 weeks to be safe
5. **Director presence**: May need to be in Indonesia for bank account
