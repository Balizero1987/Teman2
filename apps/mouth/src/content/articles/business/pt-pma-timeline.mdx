## Tips to Speed Up

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="How to Move Faster">
1. **Prepare all documents before starting** - Most delays come from incomplete docs
2. **Use experienced corporate secretary** - They know the system
3. **Be responsive** - Answer requests within 24 hours
4. **Director availability** - Plan for in-person requirements
5. **Choose bank wisely** - Some are faster than others
6. **Do parallel tasks** - Virtual office while waiting for notary, etc.
</InfoCard>
