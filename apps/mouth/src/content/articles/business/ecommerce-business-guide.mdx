## PT PMA for E-commerce

### KBLI Codes for E-commerce

| KBLI Code | Activity | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| 47911 | Internet retail (general) | Most common for online retail |
| 47912 | Wholesale internet trading | B2B e-commerce |
| 62011 | Software/app development | For platforms |
| 63122 | Web portal activities | Marketplace operations |

### Foreign Ownership

Most e-commerce activities allow **100% foreign ownership** through PT PMA. Exceptions:
- Some retail categories
- Activities on negative list
- Platform with social media elements

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Check KBLI Restrictions">
Some KBLI codes have foreign ownership limits or requirements. Verify your specific activity before setting up company.
</InfoCard>

## PSE Registration

**PSE (Penyelenggara Sistem Elektronik)** registration is mandatory for e-commerce:

### Who Needs PSE?
- Any website/app operating in Indonesia
- E-commerce platforms
- Online service providers
- Apps processing Indonesian user data

### PSE Types

| Type | For | Example |
|------|-----|---------|
| PSE Private | Private companies | E-commerce store |
| PSE Public | Government services | N/A for business |

### How to Register
1. Access OSS system
2. Apply for PSE registration
3. Provide company documents
4. Detail your electronic system
5. Receive PSE certificate

## Selling on Marketplaces

Without PT PMA, foreigners can still sell via:

### Indonesian Marketplaces
- **Tokopedia** - Largest marketplace
- **Shopee** - Popular, mobile-first
- **Lazada** - Alibaba-backed
- **Blibli** - Quality-focused
- **Bukalapak** - SME-focused

### Requirements
- Individual or business account
- Indonesian bank account (usually)
- Valid identification
- Product compliance with platform rules

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="Start Small on Marketplaces">
Test your products on marketplaces before investing in PT PMA. Use marketplace data to validate demand before larger investment.
</InfoCard>

## Tax Considerations

### E-commerce Taxation

| Tax Type | Rate | Applies To |
|----------|------|------------|
| VAT (PPN) | 11% | Sales of goods |
| Income Tax (PPh) | 22% corporate | Company profits |
| Withholding | Varies | Certain transactions |

### Cross-border Considerations
- Import duties on goods over USD 3
- VAT on digital services
- Transfer pricing rules for related parties
