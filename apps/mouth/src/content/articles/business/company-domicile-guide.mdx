## Virtual Office Explained

Virtual office is the most common choice for PT PMA:

### What You Get
- **Registered business address** for official purposes
- **Surat Keterangan Domisili** (domicile letter)
- **Mail receiving** and forwarding
- **Phone answering** (optional)
- **Meeting room access** (hourly/daily rates)

### What You Don't Get
- Physical desk or permanent space
- Daily workplace

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="Virtual Office Is Legitimate">
Virtual offices are 100% legal and accepted by:
- Ministry of Law (Kemenkumham)
- Tax office (DJP)
- OSS system
- Banks (for account opening)

Many PT PMAs operate this way, especially service-based businesses.
</InfoCard>
