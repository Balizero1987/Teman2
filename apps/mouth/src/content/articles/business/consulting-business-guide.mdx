## PT PMA for Consulting

### KBLI Codes for Consulting

| Code | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 70201 | Business management consulting | Most common |
| 70209 | Other management consulting | Catch-all |
| 62020 | IT consulting | Technology focus |
| 69209 | Other professional services | Various |
| 74909 | Other business support | Support services |

<InfoCard variant="note" title="100% Foreign Ownership Allowed">
Most consulting activities allow **100% foreign ownership**. No Indonesian partner required for standard business consulting.
</InfoCard>

### Investment Requirements

Consulting PT PMA:
- **Minimum investment**: IDR 10 billion
- **Paid-up capital**: IDR 2.5 billion minimum
- Can be lower for certain IT services

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="IT Consulting Exception">
Some IT/software consulting codes may have lower investment requirements. Check specific KBLI classifications for your service type.
</InfoCard>

## Billing & Payments

### Invoicing Indonesian Clients

With PT PMA, you can:
- Invoice in IDR
- Issue tax invoices (Faktur Pajak)
- Collect VAT (11%)
- Accept local bank transfers

### Invoicing Foreign Clients

From PT PMA:
- Invoice in USD or other currencies
- Receive to Indonesian bank account
- Convert to IDR
- Foreign exchange considerations

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="VAT on Services">
Consulting services are subject to **11% VAT (PPN)**. You must charge this to Indonesian clients and remit to tax office monthly.
</InfoCard>

---

## Work Permit Considerations

### As Director of Your PT PMA

You need:
- RPTKA (even for own company)
- DKP-TKA fee (USD 100/month)
- Director KITAS

### Hiring Other Foreign Consultants

Additional foreign staff requires:
- Individual RPTKA
- Position justification
- Indonesian counterpart training
- Full permit costs per person

---

## Tax Obligations

### Company Taxes

| Tax | Rate | Frequency |
|-----|------|-----------|
| VAT (PPN) | 11% on services | Monthly |
| Corporate Income Tax | 22% on profit | Annual |
| Withholding on payments | Varies | Per transaction |

### Personal Taxes

As director/owner:
- Salary subject to PPh 21
- Dividends taxable
- Annual SPT required

## Common Consulting Types

### Allowed for Foreigners

- Management consulting
- Strategy consulting
- IT consulting
- Financial advisory (not regulated)
- Marketing consulting
- HR consulting (non-placement)
- Technical consulting

### Restricted Areas

- Legal advice (requires Indonesian lawyer)
- Tax advice (technically restricted)
- Audit services (licensed accountants)
- Certain financial services

<InfoCard variant="note" title="Partnerships">
For restricted services, consider partnering with licensed Indonesian professionals who can provide the regulated components.
</InfoCard>

## Remote Consulting

### Working from Indonesia Without PT PMA

If clients are purely foreign:
- B211A or E2C visa options
- Invoice from foreign company
- No Indonesian employment
- Cannot serve Indonesian clients

### Limitations

- Cannot build Indonesian client base
- Bank account difficulties
- Limited long-term stability
- Visa renewals required
