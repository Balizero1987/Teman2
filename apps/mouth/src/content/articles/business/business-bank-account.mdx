## Local vs International Banks

<InfoCard variant="note" title="Which to Choose?">
**Local Banks (BCA, Mandiri):**
- More branches/ATMs
- Better local integration
- Lower fees usually
- Good for domestic business

**International Banks (HSBC, SCB):**
- Easier international transfers
- Better for global operations
- Higher minimums/fees
- English service excellent

**Most PT PMAs use:** BCA or CIMB Niaga for daily operations, sometimes international bank for cross-border.
</InfoCard>

## Opening Process

### Step by Step

| Step | What Happens |
|------|--------------|
| 1. Choose bank | Compare options, location |
| 2. Initial meeting | Discuss requirements |
| 3. Document submission | Complete package |
| 4. Due diligence | Bank verifies documents |
| 5. Approval | Usually 1-3 weeks |
| 6. Account activation | Sign documents, deposit |
| 7. Online banking setup | Token, credentials |

### Timeline

| Phase | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Document preparation | 1-2 weeks |
| Bank review | 1-3 weeks |
| Approval | 1-2 weeks |
| **Total** | **3-6 weeks** |

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Common Delays">
**What slows things down:**

- Incomplete documents
- Director without KITAS yet
- Bank compliance questions
- Address verification issues
- Shareholder documentation gaps

Prepare thoroughly to avoid delays!
</InfoCard>

## International Transfers

### Sending Money Out

<InfoCard variant="note" title="Currency Controls">
**Indonesia has some controls:**

- Transactions over USD 25K need underlying documents
- Tax clearance may be required
- Bank reports to central bank
- Proper documentation essential

Keep all invoices, contracts for transfers!
</InfoCard>

### Required Documents for Outward Transfer

| For | Documents |
|-----|-----------|
| Import payment | Invoice, LC, customs docs |
| Service payment | Contract, invoice |
| Dividend | Board resolution, tax docs |
| Loan repayment | Loan agreement |

## Receiving Money

### Inward Remittance

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Invoice/contract | Supporting document |
| NPWP | Company tax number |
| Reporting | Over USD 10K auto-reported |

### Tips for Smooth Receipt

- Inform bank of expected large transfers
- Have supporting documents ready
- Match transfer reference to invoice
- Ensure sender uses correct details

## Tips for Success

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="Account Opening Tips">
**For smooth process:**

1. **Complete documents first** - Don't submit partial
2. **Use corporate secretary** - They know bank requirements
3. **Build relationship** - Relationship manager helps
4. **Start process early** - Before you urgently need account
5. **Consider backup bank** - Two accounts is common
6. **Keep documents updated** - Annual deed changes
</InfoCard>

### Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Consequence |
|---------|-------------|
| Incomplete documents | Rejection/delays |
| Director without KITAS | Cannot open |
| Wrong company name | Must match exactly |
| Old deed version | Must be current |
| Missing NPWP | Immediate rejection |
