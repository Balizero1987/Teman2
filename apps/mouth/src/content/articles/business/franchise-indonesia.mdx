## Franchise Regulations

### Key Requirements

<InfoCard variant="note" title="Government Registration">
**Franchises must be registered:**

- STPW (Surat Tanda Pendaftaran Waralaba)
- Required for franchisor
- Register within 30 days of starting
- Renewal required

Operating without registration is illegal.
</InfoCard>

### Regulatory Framework

| Regulation | Covers |
|------------|--------|
| Gov Reg 42/2007 | Franchise definition, requirements |
| Minister Reg 71/2019 | Registration procedures |
| Trade Minister | Ongoing oversight |

## Foreign Franchises

### Bringing Foreign Brand to Indonesia

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Foreign Franchise Rules">
**Requirements for foreign brands:**

- Master franchise agreement
- PT PMA typically required
- Register franchise in Indonesia
- Local partner may be needed
- Localization requirements
- Local sourcing rules (80% for certain items)

Foreign franchisors must adapt to Indonesian regulations.
</InfoCard>

### Local Content Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Local products | 80% for certain F&B items |
| Local partnership | May be required for some sectors |
| Local management | Indonesian involvement |
| Registration | In Indonesia |

## Franchisee Considerations

### Before Buying

| Factor | Check |
|--------|-------|
| Franchisor track record | Years in business, success rate |
| Financial requirements | Total investment, working capital |
| Territory rights | Exclusive? Protected? |
| Fees | Initial, ongoing royalties |
| Support provided | Training, marketing, operations |
| Exit options | Can you sell? Transfer? |

### Typical Fee Structure

| Fee Type | Typical Range |
|----------|---------------|
| Franchise fee | IDR 50M - 500M+ |
| Royalty | 3-8% of revenue |
| Marketing fee | 1-3% of revenue |
| Equipment | Variable |
| Initial inventory | Variable |
| Working capital | 3-6 months operating costs |

## STPW Registration

### Process for Franchisors

| Step | Duration |
|------|----------|
| Prepare documents | 2-4 weeks |
| Submit application | 1 week |
| Review by Trade Ministry | 2-4 weeks |
| STPW issued | Upon approval |
| Valid for | 5 years |

### Required Documents

| Document | Details |
|----------|---------|
| Franchise prospectus | Business details |
| Franchise agreement | Sample contract |
| Proof of IP ownership | Trademark certificates |
| Company documents | Legal status |
| Operational manuals | System documentation |
