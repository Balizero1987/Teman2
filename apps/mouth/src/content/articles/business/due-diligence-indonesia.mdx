## Operational Due Diligence

### Key Areas

| Area | What to Check |
|------|---------------|
| Management | Key person dependency, competence |
| Employees | Skills, turnover, disputes |
| Systems | IT, processes, scalability |
| Facilities | Condition, capacity, compliance |
| Suppliers | Dependency, alternatives, contracts |
| Customers | Satisfaction, loyalty, concentration |

### Red Flags

| Red Flag | Concern |
|----------|---------|
| Key person dependency | Risk if they leave |
| High employee turnover | Culture/management issues |
| Aging technology | Future investment needed |
| Single supplier dependency | Supply risk |
| Customer concentration | Revenue risk |
| Poor documentation | Control weaknesses |

## Common DD Findings

<InfoCard variant="note" title="Typical Issues Found">
**In Indonesian companies:**

1. **License gaps** - Missing or expired permits
2. **Labor violations** - BPJS, overtime, contracts
3. **Tax underpayment** - Especially withholding
4. **Related party issues** - Undisclosed, unfavorable terms
5. **Land title problems** - Unclear ownership, disputes
6. **Environmental non-compliance** - No permits, violations
7. **Undisclosed liabilities** - Hidden debts, guarantees
8. **IP not registered** - Trademark, patent gaps
</InfoCard>

## DD Process

### Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Activities |
|-------|----------|------------|
| Planning | 1 week | Scope, team, access |
| Document collection | 1-2 weeks | Request and receive |
| Analysis | 2-4 weeks | Review, verify |
| Site visits | 1 week | Physical inspection |
| Reporting | 1 week | Findings, recommendations |
| Q&A | 1-2 weeks | Clarifications |

### Team Structure

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Deal lead | Overall coordination |
| Legal counsel | Legal DD |
| Accountant | Financial DD |
| Tax advisor | Tax DD |

| Local consultant | Indonesia-specific |

## Dealing with Findings

### Response Options

| Finding | Options |
|---------|---------|
| Deal breaker | Walk away |
| Material issue | Price adjustment |
| Fixable issue | Require remedy before closing |
| Risk allocation | Indemnity in SPA |
| Minor issue | Accept risk |

### Indemnification

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="Protect Yourself">
**For identified risks:**

- Specific indemnities in sale agreement
- Escrow holdback amounts
- Earn-out tied to representations
- Insurance (reps & warranties)
- Closing conditions requiring remedy

Don't just accept risks - allocate them properly!
</InfoCard>
