# Tech Startup in Indonesia 2025: Guide for Foreign Founders

Indonesia is Southeast Asia's largest startup ecosystem with unicorns like Gojek, Tokopedia, and Traveloka. Here's how foreign founders can build here.

## Why Indonesia for Tech?

- **270 million** population
- **200+ million** internet users
- **Median age 29** - young, tech-savvy
- **Growing middle class**
- **Mobile-first market**
- **Active VC ecosystem**

## Hiring in Indonesia

### Local Talent

- Strong engineering talent, especially in Jakarta
- Lower costs than Singapore
- Growing pool of experienced startup employees
- English proficiency varies

### Employment Basics

| Requirement | Notes |
|-------------|-------|
| BPJS Kesehatan | Health insurance (mandatory) |
| BPJS Ketenagakerjaan | Employment insurance (mandatory) |
| Minimum wage | By province (Jakarta ~IDR 5M/month) |
| Contracts | Fixed-term or permanent |
| Severance | Significant for layoffs |

### Foreign Employees

Hiring foreign tech talent:
- RPTKA required per position
- USD 100/month DKP-TKA fee
- Must train Indonesian counterpart
- Generally limited to senior/specialist roles

## Tech-Specific Regulations

### PSE (Electronic System Operator)

All tech platforms must register:
- Mandatory for operating in Indonesia
- Data localization requirements
- Content moderation obligations
- Government access provisions

### Data Protection

- Indonesia has personal data protection law
- Requirements for data handling
- Consent and security obligations
- Penalties for non-compliance

### Fintech Specific

If building fintech:
- OJK licensing required
- Sandbox programs available
- Capital requirements
- Regular reporting
