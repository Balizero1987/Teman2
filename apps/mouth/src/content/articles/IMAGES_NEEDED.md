## Image Generation Options

### AI Generation (Recommended)
- **Midjourney/DALL-E**: For original, rights-free images
- **Prompt template**: `[subject], professional photography, Indonesia/Bali setting, modern, editorial style --ar 16:9`

### Stock Photos
- **Unsplash**: Free, high-quality
- **Pexels**: Free, good Indonesia selection
- **Shutterstock**: Paid, professional quality

### Naming Convention
```
{category}-{slug}.jpg
```

Example: `immigration-visa-on-arrival.jpg`
