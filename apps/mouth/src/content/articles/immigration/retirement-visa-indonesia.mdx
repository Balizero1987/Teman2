# Retirement Visa in Indonesia 2026: Complete Guide

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Aggiornamento 2026 (Fonte: imigrasi.go.id)">
**Sistema Ibrido Retirement KITAS (dal maggio 2025):**

- **Fase Online**: Domanda iniziale e upload documenti su imigrasi.go.id
- **Fase In-Person**: Biometrie obbligatorie presso ufficio immigrazione locale
- **Requisiti età confermati**: Entrambi i tipi (E33E e E33F) richiedono 55+ anni

I tempi di elaborazione variano: 2-4 settimane per la maggior parte delle domande.
</InfoCard>

Indonesia offers **two retirement visa options** for foreigners who want to enjoy their golden years in beautiful locations like Bali:

| Visa | Code | Age | Duration | Deposit | Monthly Income | Bali Zero Price |
|------|------|-----|----------|---------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Retirement KITAS** | E33F | 55+ | 1 year | None | USD 1,500 | IDR 14,000,000 |
| **Retirement KITAS** | E33E | 55+ | 5 years | USD 50,000 | USD 3,000 | IDR 18,000,000 |

<InfoCard variant="highlight" title="Which One Should You Choose?">
**E33F Retirement KITAS (55+, 1 year)**: No deposit required, lower income threshold (USD 1,500/mo). Annual renewal required. Best if you have a smaller budget.

**E33E Retirement KITAS (55+, 5 years)**: Requires USD 50,000 deposit but 5 years of hassle-free residency. Best if you have savings and want convenience.

Both require age 55+ (verified from imigrasi.go.id) and lead to **KITAP (permanent residence)** after 5 consecutive years.
</InfoCard>

## Option 1: E33F Retirement KITAS (55+)

The E33F is ideal for retirees aged 55-59 or those who prefer lower upfront costs with annual renewals.

### E33F Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Age** | 55 years or older |
| **Monthly Income** | USD 1,500+ (pension, investments, etc.) |
| **Sponsor** | Required (visa agent can sponsor) |
| **Deposit** | None required |
| **Passport** | Valid min 18 months |
| **Insurance** | Health coverage valid in Indonesia |

### E33F Costs

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| **First Year** | IDR 14,000,000 (~USD 900) |
| **Annual Renewal** | IDR 11,000,000 (~USD 700) |
| **5-Year Total** | ~IDR 58,000,000 (~USD 3,700) |

### E33F Benefits
- Lower age requirement (55+ vs 60+)
- No large upfront deposit required
- Sponsor handles all paperwork
- Multiple entry (travel freely)
- Path to KITAP after 5 years

---

## Option 2: E33E Retirement KITAS (55+, 5 Years)

The E33E is ideal for retirees 55+ who want 5 years of hassle-free residency and have savings to deposit.

### E33E Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Age** | 55 years or older |
| **Monthly Income** | USD 3,000+ (pension, investments, etc.) |
| **Bank Deposit** | USD 50,000 at Indonesian state bank |
| **Sponsor** | Not required (apply directly) |
| **Passport** | Valid min 6 years |
| **Insurance** | Health coverage valid in Indonesia |

### E33E Costs (Bali Zero)

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| **5-Year Visa** | IDR 18,000,000 |
| **Bank Deposit** | USD 50,000 (refundable) |
| **Total 5-Year Cost** | IDR 18,000,000 (deposit remains yours) |

### E33E Benefits
- 5 years without annual renewals
- No sponsor required
- Lower total processing cost
- Multiple entry unlimited
- Path to KITAP after 5 years

<InfoCard variant="note" title="USD 50,000 Deposit">
The deposit must be made at an Indonesian **state bank**:
- BNI (Bank Negara Indonesia)
- BRI (Bank Rakyat Indonesia)
- Mandiri
- BTN (Bank Tabungan Negara)

The money **remains yours** - you can use it for living expenses or withdraw after the visa period.
</InfoCard>

## Rights and Limitations

### What You CAN Do
- Live in Indonesia long-term
- Travel in and out freely (multiple entry)
- Open Indonesian bank account
- Rent or buy property (leasehold)
- Sponsor spouse/dependents
- Enjoy retirement in paradise!

### What You CANNOT Do
- Work (any kind of employment)
- Run a business
- Receive Indonesian income
- Engage in commercial activities

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="No Work Allowed">
Both retirement visas are strictly non-work. Any employment or business activity violates the visa conditions and can result in deportation.
</InfoCard>

---

## Path to KITAP (Permanent Residence)

After **5 consecutive years** on either E33F or E33E, you may apply for **KITAP**:

| KITAP Benefits |
|----------------|
| 5-year validity (renewable) |
| More stability and permanence |
| Simpler renewal process |
| No more annual KITAS renewals |

**E33F Path**: 5 annual renewals → KITAP
**E33E Path**: 1 x 5-year visa → KITAP

---

## Spouse and Dependents

### Spouse Options

**If spouse is also 55+/60+:**
- Apply for their own E33F or E33E
- Same requirements apply

**If spouse is younger:**
- Apply for **Dependent KITAS** under your visa
- Cost: IDR 11,000,000/year
- Tied to your visa validity

### Required Documents for Spouse
- Marriage certificate (legalized)
- Passport and photos
- Health insurance
- Medical fitness certificate

## Decision Guide

**Choose E33F Retirement KITAS if:**
- You're 55+ years old
- You prefer lower upfront costs
- Your pension is USD 1,500-2,999/month
- You don't have USD 50,000 to deposit
- You're okay with annual renewals

**Choose E33E Retirement KITAS if:**
- You're 55+ years old
- You want 5 years without renewal hassle
- Your pension is USD 3,000+/month
- You have USD 50,000 savings to deposit
- You prefer convenience over cost savings

### Possibili sviluppi visti pensionati (OPINIONE)

1. **Golden Visa per pensionati** - Si discute se il programma Golden Visa (attualmente focalizzato su investitori con $2.5M+) potrebbe in futuro includere una categoria speciale per pensionati con patrimonio elevato ma senza obbligo di investimento attivo. *Nessun annuncio ufficiale.*

2. **Riduzione deposito E33E** - Alcuni operatori del settore ritengono che il deposito di USD 50,000 per l'E33E potrebbe essere rivisto per renderlo più competitivo con programmi simili in Thailandia e Malesia. *Solo speculazione.*

3. **Healthcare obbligatoria BPJS** - C'è discussione sulla possibile estensione dell'obbligo di iscrizione a BPJS Kesehatan (assicurazione sanitaria nazionale) anche per titolari di visti pensionati. *Non confermato.*

4. **Percorso accelerato verso KITAP** - Si discute di un possibile percorso accelerato verso la residenza permanente (KITAP) per pensionati che dimostrano contributi significativi all'economia locale. *Solo discussione accademica.*
