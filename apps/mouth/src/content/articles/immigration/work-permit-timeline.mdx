### System & Timing Delays

- **Public holidays** - No processing during Lebaran, Christmas, etc.
- **System maintenance** - TKA Online sometimes down
- **High volume periods** - Slower during peak times
- **Company verification** - If company hasn't used system before

### Employer-Side Delays

- **RPTKA clarification requests** - Ministry may ask questions
- **Payment delays** - DKP-TKA must be paid to proceed
- **Wrong position classification** - May need to reapply
- **Missing company documents** - NIB, NPWP, etc.

## Planning Tips

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="Start Early">
1. **Begin 3 months before** planned start date
2. **Prepare documents** while employer handles RPTKA
3. **Don't book flights** until VITAS approved
4. **Apply for extension** 30 days before expiry
</InfoCard>

### Critical Deadlines

| Action | Deadline |
|--------|----------|
| KITAS extension application | 30 days before expiry |
| Exit if permit denied | Before visa expires |
| SKTT registration | 14 days after KITAS |
| Report address change | 7 days |

## Can You Speed It Up?

### What You CAN'T Rush
- RPTKA review (government sets pace)
- System processing times
- Biometrics scheduling

### What You CAN Optimize
- Document preparation (start early)
- Agent selection (experienced = faster)
- Company readiness (update NIB beforehand)
- Complete application (no missing items)

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="No Official Rush Service">
There's no official "express" work permit process. Anyone promising guaranteed fast-tracking should be viewed skeptically. Experienced agents are faster because they avoid mistakes, not because they have special access.
</InfoCard>
