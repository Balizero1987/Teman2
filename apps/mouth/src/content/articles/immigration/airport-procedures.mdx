## VOA Payment

### At VOA Counter

<InfoCard variant="note" title="Visa on Arrival">
**Before immigration, pay for VOA:**

- Cost: IDR 500,000
- Payment: Cash (IDR, USD) or card
- Receipt: Keep for immigration
- Duration: 30 days, extendable

Look for "Visa on Arrival" counter before the immigration queues.
</InfoCard>

### Payment Options

| Method | Accepted |
|--------|----------|
| Indonesian Rupiah | Yes |
| US Dollars | Yes |
| Credit/Debit Card | Yes (most airports) |
| Other currencies | Some accepted |

## Immigration

### What to Expect

| Item | What Happens |
|------|--------------|
| Documents | Show passport + VOA receipt/visa |
| Questions | May ask purpose, duration, address |
| Biometrics | Photo and fingerprints |
| Stamp | Entry stamp in passport |
| Duration | Varies (can be quick or slow) |

### Common Questions Asked

| Question | Good Answer |
|----------|-------------|
| Purpose of visit? | Tourism / Business meetings / Visiting family |
| How long staying? | [Your actual planned duration] |
| Where staying? | [Hotel name and area] |
| Return flight? | [Date and airline] |
| Occupation? | [Your job/profession] |

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="Immigration Tips">
**For smooth passage:**

- Be polite and patient
- Answer truthfully
- Have accommodation address ready
- Know your return flight date
- Don't joke about illegal things
- Dress reasonably
</InfoCard>

## Customs

### What to Declare

| Declare | Allowance |
|---------|-----------|
| Cash over IDR 100M / USD 10K | Must declare |
| Cigarettes over 200 | Must declare |
| Alcohol over 1 liter | Must declare |
| Goods over USD 500 value | May be dutiable |

### Prohibited Items

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Do Not Bring">
**Strictly prohibited:**
- Narcotics/drugs (death penalty!)
- Weapons
- Pornography
- Counterfeit goods
- Certain food products
- Items offensive to religion

Drugs carry **death penalty** in Indonesia!
</InfoCard>

### Channels

| Channel | Use When |
|---------|----------|
| Green | Nothing to declare |
| Red | Items to declare |

You may still be randomly checked in green channel.

## Overstay Check

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Check Before Departure">
**Before going to immigration:**

- Count your days in Indonesia
- Check visa expiry date
- Overstay = IDR 1,000,000 per day fine
- Fines paid at airport immigration
- Can delay your departure

Calculate carefully to avoid surprises!
</InfoCard>
