## Tax Holiday

### What Is It?

<InfoCard variant="note" title="Full CIT Exemption">
**Tax Holiday provides:**

- 100% Corporate Income Tax exemption
- For 5-20 years depending on investment
- 50% reduction for 2 years after
- For pioneer industries

This is the most generous incentive available.
</InfoCard>

### Eligibility

| Requirement | Criteria |
|-------------|----------|
| Pioneer industry | Specific list of sectors |
| Minimum investment | IDR 100 billion+ (some sectors) |
| New investment | Not expansion of existing |
| Legal entity | PT PMA or PT |
| Plans | Business plan approved |

### Duration Based on Investment

| Investment (IDR) | Holiday Period |
|------------------|----------------|
| 100B - 500B | 5 years |
| 500B - 1T | 7 years |
| 1T - 5T | 10 years |
| 5T - 15T | 15 years |
| Over 15T | 20 years |

### Pioneer Industries Include

- Basic metals
- Oil refining
- Petrochemicals
- Industrial machinery
- Telecommunications
- Digital economy infrastructure
- Certain others

## Tax Allowance

### What Is It?

<InfoCard variant="note" title="Investment Deduction">
**Tax Allowance provides:**

- 30% additional deduction on investment
- Over 6 years (5% per year)
- Accelerated depreciation
- Loss carry forward up to 10 years
- Reduced withholding on dividends

More accessible than Tax Holiday for smaller investments.
</InfoCard>

### Eligible Sectors

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Manufacturing | Various industries |
| Agriculture | Plantations, farming |
| Tourism | Hotels, attractions |
| Infrastructure | Power, transport |
| Special economic zones | Location-based |

## Special Economic Zones (KEK)

### Zone Benefits

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="KEK Incentives">
**Special Economic Zones offer:**

- Tax Holiday availability
- Import duty exemptions
- Simplified permits
- Infrastructure support
- Cluster benefits

Consider KEK locations for manufacturing and export.
</InfoCard>

### Major KEKs

|------|----------|-------|
| Sei Mangkei | North Sumatra | Palm oil, manufacturing |
| Tanjung Lesung | Banten | Tourism |
| Mandalika | Lombok | Tourism |
| Galang Batang | Bintan | Manufacturing |
| Kendal | Central Java | Manufacturing |

## SME Benefits

### Small Business Rate

<InfoCard variant="note" title="0.5% Final Tax">
**For small businesses:**

- Revenue under IDR 4.8 billion/year
- Pay 0.5% of revenue (not profit)
- Final tax (simple compliance)
- Can continue until outgrow threshold

Significant benefit for starting businesses.
</InfoCard>
