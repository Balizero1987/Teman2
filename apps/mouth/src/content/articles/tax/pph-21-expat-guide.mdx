# Income Tax (PPh 21) for Expatriates in Indonesia 2025

PPh 21 is the income tax withheld from your salary by your employer. If you work in Indonesia, this is your primary tax obligation.

## PTKP (Tax-Free Allowance)

PTKP is the amount of income exempt from tax:

| Status | Code | Annual PTKP |
|--------|------|-------------|
| Single | TK/0 | IDR 54,000,000 |
| Married, no kids | K/0 | IDR 58,500,000 |
| Married + 1 child | K/1 | IDR 63,000,000 |
| Married + 2 children | K/2 | IDR 67,500,000 |
| Married + 3 children | K/3 | IDR 72,000,000 |

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="Maximize Your PTKP">
- Register dependents properly with HR
- Maximum 3 dependents (children)
- Dependents must live with you
- Spouse only counts if not working
</InfoCard>

## What's Taxable?

### Included in PPh 21:
- Base salary
- Allowances (housing, transport, meals)
- Bonuses and THR
- Benefits in kind (some)
- Stock options (when exercised)

### Exempt:
- BPJS employer contributions
- Certain work tools/equipment
- Some business travel reimbursements

---

## No NPWP = Higher Tax

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="20% Penalty Without NPWP">
If you don't have NPWP, your employer withholds **20% more** tax. This adds up quickly:

- With NPWP: IDR 50M tax
- Without NPWP: IDR 60M tax

Get your NPWP to avoid this penalty.
</InfoCard>
