## SME Final Tax (PP 55/2022)

<InfoCard variant="tip" title="Simplified for Small Business">
Businesses with gross turnover under **IDR 4.8 billion/year** can use simplified final tax:

- **Rate**: 0.5% of gross turnover
- **No complex calculations**
- **Monthly payment**
- **Applies to**: PT, CV, individuals with business
- **Duration**: Maximum 7 years for PT, 4 years for CV/individuals

After the maximum period, regular rates apply.
</InfoCard>

### SME Tax Example

| Monthly Revenue | Tax Due |
|-----------------|---------|
| IDR 100M | IDR 500K |
| IDR 200M | IDR 1M |
| IDR 400M | IDR 2M |

## Tax Filing Requirements

### Annual Corporate Tax Return

<InfoCard variant="note" title="Filing Deadlines">
**SPT Tahunan Badan** (Annual Corporate Tax Return):
- **Deadline**: 4 months after fiscal year-end
- **For calendar year**: April 30
- **Extension**: Up to 2 months possible
- **Method**: e-Filing mandatory
</InfoCard>

### Monthly Obligations

| Obligation | Deadline | Description |
|------------|----------|-------------|
| PPh 21 | 10th of following month | Employee withholding |
| PPh 23 | 10th of following month | Service withholding |
| PPh 25 | 15th of following month | Prepaid CIT installment |
| PPh 4(2) | 10th of following month | Final tax items |
| VAT (PPN) | End of following month | If VAT registered |

## Transfer Pricing

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Related Party Transactions">
Indonesia has strict **transfer pricing** rules for transactions between related parties:

- Must be at **arm's length** prices
- **Documentation required** (Master File, Local File)
- **Country-by-Country Reporting** for large groups
- **Penalties** for non-compliance

Work with qualified tax advisors for related party transactions.
</InfoCard>
