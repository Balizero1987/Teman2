# Scooter Rental Indonesia 2026: Complete Guide

Scooters are the most practical way to get around in Bali and many parts of Indonesia. Here's what you need to know.

## License Requirements

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="You Need a License">
**Legal requirement:**

To legally ride a scooter in Indonesia, you need:
- **International Driving Permit (IDP)** with motorcycle endorsement, OR
- **Indonesian driving license (SIM C)**

Riding without proper license:
- Police can fine you (IDR 250K-500K)
- Insurance may be void
- Liability issues if accident

Many tourists ride without - but it's a risk.
</InfoCard>

### License Options

| Option | For | Duration |
|--------|-----|----------|
| IDP | Short-term visitors | 1 year |
| SIM C | KITAS holders | 5 years |
| Tourist SIM C | Some areas offer | Limited |

## Safety Tips

### Essential Safety

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Scooter Accidents">
**#1 risk for tourists in Bali**

Scooter accidents cause more tourist injuries than anything else.

**Stay safe:**
- Always wear helmet (properly fastened)
- Don't drink and ride
- Watch for sand, gravel, potholes
- Assume others won't stop
- Don't speed
- Be extra careful at night
- Use sunscreen (arms burn!)
</InfoCard>

### Riding Tips

| Situation | What to Do |
|-----------|------------|
| Intersections | Slow down, be ready |
| Overtaking | Use horn, go slowly |
| At night | Avoid if possible |
| Rain | Pull over and wait |
| Dogs | Slow down, don't swerve |
| Tourist areas | Watch for pedestrians |
| Mountains | Gear down, use brakes |

## Traffic Rules

### Key Rules

| Rule | Note |
|------|------|
| Drive on left | Like UK, Australia |
| Helmet required | By law (enforced) |
| Max 2 riders | Unless small children |
| Use indicators | Signal turns |
| No phone | While riding |
| Carry documents | STNK, license |

## Insurance

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Insurance Matters">
**Critical:** Travel insurance may NOT cover you if:

- You don't have valid license
- You're riding without helmet
- You're under influence
- You're on non-paved roads

**Check your policy carefully!**

Consider specific motorbike coverage if riding regularly.
</InfoCard>

### Insurance Options

| Type | Coverage |
|------|----------|
| Travel insurance | May cover with license |
| Rental insurance | Usually third-party only |
| Separate bike policy | Comprehensive option |
| Credit card | Check terms carefully |
