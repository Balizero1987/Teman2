# Banking for Foreigners in Indonesia 2026: Complete Guide

Having an Indonesian bank account makes life much easier. Here's how foreigners can open accounts and manage money.

## Can Foreigners Open Bank Accounts?

<InfoCard variant="note" title="Yes, With Requirements">
Foreigners can open Indonesian bank accounts if they have:
- Valid passport
- **KITAS or KITAP** (for full accounts)
- **NPWP** (tax number - often required)
- Proof of address in Indonesia

Some banks allow limited accounts for tourists, but full services require residency.
</InfoCard>

## Banking Without KITAS

If you don't have KITAS:

### Options
1. **Tourist savings accounts** - Limited, some banks offer
2. **Digital banks** - Some allow passport-only
3. **International banks** - HSBC with global relationship
4. **Payment apps** - GoPay, OVO (limited functions)

<InfoCard variant="warning" title="Limited Without KITAS">
Without KITAS, banking options are very limited. Consider:
- Using international cards (Wise, Revolut)
- Withdrawing from ATMs with foreign cards
- Digital payment apps for daily expenses
</InfoCard>

### Receiving Money to Indonesia

| Method | Speed | Cost | Notes |

| **Bank wire** | 2-5 days | High | Traditional |

- May need to show source of funds
- Tax implications possible
