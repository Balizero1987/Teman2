## Accommodation Costs

### Monthly Rental Ranges

| Type | Monthly Range (IDR) | USD | Notes |
|------|---------------------|-----|-------|
| Kos (room) | 1.5-4M | $100-250 | Basic, local area |
| Studio apartment | 5-10M | $300-600 | With kitchen |
| 1BR apartment | 8-15M | $500-900 | In good area |
| 2BR villa | 12-25M | $750-1,500 | Pool, garden |
| 3BR+ villa | 20-50M+ | $1,250-3,000+ | Family/premium |

## Money-Saving Tips

1. **Eat local** - Warungs are delicious and cheap
2. **Long-term rental** - Negotiate for 6-12 months
3. **Buy a scooter** - Cheaper than renting long-term
4. **Cook sometimes** - Markets have great produce
5. **Off-peak** - Avoid peak season for accommodation
6. **Local SIM** - Much cheaper than roaming
7. **Bintang over imports** - Local beer is affordable
8. **Free beaches** - Skip expensive beach clubs
