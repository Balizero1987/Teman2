## What is NPWP?

**NPWP** (Nomor Pokok Wajib Pajak) is Indonesia's Tax Identification Number. There are two types:

1. **NPWP Personal** - For individuals (employees, freelancers, foreigners)
2. **NPWP Badan/Corporate** - For companies (PT, CV, PT PMA)

## NPWP Personal

### Who Needs It:
- Indonesian citizens with income
- Foreigners with KITAS and Indonesian income
- Directors of PT PMA companies
- Freelancers and self-employed

### Requirements:
- KTP (Indonesian ID) or KITAS/KITAP
- Passport (for foreigners)
- Domicile letter (Surat Keterangan Domisili)
- SKTT if foreigner

### Registration Process:
1. **Prepare documents** - ID, domicile, KITAS
2. **Apply at KPP** (Kantor Pelayanan Pajak) - local tax office
3. **Or online** via ereg.pajak.go.id
4. **Receive NPWP card** - 2-3 days

## NPWP Badan (Corporate)

### Who Needs It:
- PT PMA (Foreign Investment Company)
- PT Lokal (Local Company)
- CV (Partnership)
- All registered businesses

### Requirements:
- Akta pendirian perusahaan (company deed)
- SK Kemenkumham (Ministry approval)
- NIB (Nomor Induk Berusaha)
- KTP/KITAS directors
- NPWP directors (personal)
- Office address proof

### Registration Process:
1. **Company establishment completed** - Akta, SK, NIB
2. **Apply at KPP** or via ereg.pajak.go.id
3. **Verification visit** by tax officer (sometimes)
4. **NPWP issued** - 3-5 working days

## NPWPD (Regional Tax ID)

### What is NPWPD?
Regional tax registration number for local taxes (PDRD - Pajak Daerah dan Retribusi Daerah).

### Required for:
- Hotel tax (PHR)
- Restaurant tax
- Entertainment tax
- Parking tax
- Advertisement tax

### Registration:
- At Bapenda (local revenue office)
- After NIB and NPWP obtained
- Specific to business location (kabupaten/kota)

## Tax Residency Rules

### When are you a Tax Resident?
- Present in Indonesia **183+ days per year**
- Intend to reside in Indonesia

### Tax Resident Obligations:
- File SPT (annual tax return) on worldwide income
- NPWP mandatory
- Monthly tax deposits if self-employed

### Non-Resident:
- Taxed only on Indonesian-source income
- 20% withholding tax rate
- No SPT filing required

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need NPWP for KITAS?**
A: Yes, Working KITAS (E23/E28) requires NPWP. Remote Worker (E33G) generally does not.

**Q: Can I open bank account without NPWP?**
A: Limited. Most banks require NPWP for significant transactions or business accounts.

**Q: What if I don't file taxes?**
A: Penalties 2% per month + potential tax audit. Keep compliant!

**Q: How long is NPWP valid?**
A: Permanently valid unless formally deregistered.

**Q: Can I deregister NPWP when leaving Indonesia?**
A: Yes, submit closure form at KPP. Must clear all tax obligations first.

**Tax Residency (183 days):**
