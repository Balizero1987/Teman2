## What is BPJS?

**BPJS** (Badan Penyelenggara Jaminan Sosial) is Indonesia's mandatory social security system with two programs:

1. **BPJS Kesehatan** - National Health Insurance
2. **BPJS Ketenagakerjaan** - Employment Insurance (work accident, death, pension)

## Who Needs BPJS?

### Mandatory for:
- All KITAS holders (6+ months validity)
- All PT PMA companies with employees
- Directors listed in company structure
- Indonesian employees

### KITAS Holders:
- **Cannot extend KITAS** without BPJS payment proof
- Required from first month of KITAS validity
- Monthly premium based on salary class

## BPJS Kesehatan (Health Insurance)

### Coverage:
- Hospital inpatient care
- Outpatient consultations
- Emergency treatment
- Medications
- Laboratory tests
- Specialist referrals

### Class System:
| Class | Room Type | Monthly Premium |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| Class 1 | Private room | IDR 150,000 |
| Class 2 | Shared (2 beds) | IDR 100,000 |
| Class 3 | Shared (4+ beds) | IDR 42,000 |

### How to Use:
1. Visit **Puskesmas** (public health center) as first level
2. Get referral to **RS** (hospital) for specialist
3. Show BPJS card at registration
4. No upfront payment for covered services

## BPJS Ketenagakerjaan (Employment Insurance)

### Programs Included:

1. **JKK** (Jaminan Kecelakaan Kerja) - Work Accident Insurance
   - 0.24% - 1.74% of salary (paid by employer)
   - Covers work-related injuries and medical

2. **JKM** (Jaminan Kematian) - Death Benefit
   - 0.30% of salary (paid by employer)
   - Lump sum to family if worker dies

3. **JHT** (Jaminan Hari Tua) - Old Age Savings
   - 5.7% of salary (3.7% employer + 2% worker)
   - Claimable upon retirement or leaving Indonesia

4. **JP** (Jaminan Pensiun) - Pension
   - 3% of salary (2% employer + 1% worker)
   - Monthly pension payments after retirement

## Registration Process

### For New PT PMA:

1. **Prepare documents:**
   - NIB (Nomor Induk Berusaha)
   - Akta pendirian perusahaan
   - NPWP perusahaan
   - KTP/KITAS directors and employees
   - Salary slip/employment contracts

2. **Register at BPJS office:**
   - BPJS Kesehatan: bpjs-kesehatan.go.id
   - BPJS Ketenagakerjaan: bpjsketenagakerjaan.go.id

3. **Monthly payments:**
   - Set up auto-debit or manual payment
   - Due by 10th of each month

## Important Warnings

**BPJS Mandatory Rule:**
- Companies with 10+ employees OR payroll > IDR 1M/month MUST enroll
- Failure to enroll = fines + cannot obtain government services

**KITAS Extension:**
- Immigration requires **BPJS payment proof** for KITAS extension
- Cannot extend permit without valid BPJS status

**Penalty for Late Payment:**
- 2% fine per month
- Services suspended after 1 month non-payment

## FAQ

**Q: Can foreigners use BPJS?**
A: Yes, KITAS holders are required to have BPJS Kesehatan. Coverage is same as Indonesian citizens.

**Q: What if my KITAS expires?**
A: BPJS membership suspended. Reactivate when new KITAS issued.

**Q: Can I have private insurance instead?**
A: BPJS is mandatory regardless. You can have private insurance additionally.

**Q: How long to get BPJS card?**
A: 2-3 weeks after successful registration.
